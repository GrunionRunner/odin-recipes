Grunion Taco

The grunion taco is a So-Cal cuisine made of filleted grunion which is seasoned with lime juice, cumin, and chili sauce.

The grunion taco can also be topped with avocados, red cabbage, cilantro, and onions.

Ingredients

  • Grunions
  • tortilla
  • lime juice
  • cumin
  • chili powder or chili sauce

Steps

  1. Fillet the grunion
  2. lightly dress in oil
  3. Set the filleted grunion in a baking dish
  4. Season with a blend of lime juice, cumin, and chili powder/sauce
  5. Bake grunion at 300 degrees fahrenheit for 10 to 15 minutes
  6. Place everything into tortilla
