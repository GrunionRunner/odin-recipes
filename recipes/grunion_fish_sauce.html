Grunion Fish Sauce

A fish sauce made from the humble Grunion. The process for grunion fish sauce is a long and arduous process.

Ingredients

  • Grunion
  • Sea salt
  • whey/pickle brine
  • cheesecloth

Steps

  1. Cut 1.5lbs of grunion into small pieces
  2. Place into jar and mash the grunion
  3. Add 3 tbsp of sea salt and 2 tbsp of brine
  4. Fill the rest of jar with water and mix
  5. Wait for 3 months while periodically relieving pressure built by the fermentation process
  6. After 3 months, strain with cheesecloth to remove solids. The liquid is now the fish sauce
